With Profound Gratitude to Our Community

The story of Crystal Springs Uplands School is, at its heart, a story of collective investment. To the current families who entrust us with their children’s growth, the alumni who carry our values into the global arena, and the trusted friends who believe in our mission: we thank you for your individual contributions.

True philanthropic impact is never measured by the size of a single ledger entry, but by the shared commitment of our entire network. Every donation, no matter the amount, acts as an active catalyst. Your support directly sustains our specialized academic programs, expands equitable accessibility through critical tuition assistance foundations, and preserves our historic campus learning spaces.

Because of your combined efforts, Crystal students do not merely navigate their educational journeys—they continue to achieve, challenge boundaries, and truly thrive as kind, curious, compassionate global citizens.

Happy, challenged, and successful — kind, curious, and compassionate global citizens.
